> [!quote]
> "They say the essence of the master is the only cure for the slave. But in the City of Westgate, who is truly the master?"

## Discovery

Lucian found cryptic mentions of this theory in the restricted archives of the **Order of the Gauntlet**. It posits that a **Dhampir** can cleanse their blood of the vampiric curse by slaying the one who turned them and consuming their essence in a specific ritual.

## The Ritual

The texts are intentionally vague, likely to discourage such a dark act. However, three components are consistently mentioned:

1.  **The Heart of the Progenitor**: Must be taken while the vampire is in a state of true death.
2.  **A Vessel of Pure Silver**: Luthcheq-forged silver is recommended for its anti-magical properties.
3.  **A Prayer to Assuran**: Justice must be sought as a form of Retribution.
